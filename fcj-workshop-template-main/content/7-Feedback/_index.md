> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey program. This will help the FCAJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment at AWS Vietnam is highly professional, tech-driven, yet very open. Team members are always ready to help when I struggle with Cloud services. The comfortable workspace allows me to focus intensely while deploying systems.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentors are incredibly enthusiastic, especially regarding complex AWS architecture concepts (VPC, ALB, RDS) or when I get stuck configuring CloudFront. They don't just hand over solutions; they teach me how to troubleshoot using system logs.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The Rookwork project perfectly bridges my university knowledge (React, Spring Boot) with practical Cloud infrastructure on AWS. This experience has deepened my understanding of how real-world software is operated and scaled.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, I learned how to manage IAM permissions and design multi-platform software. Furthermore, the specialized workshops hosted by FCAJ upgraded my mindset regarding security and infrastructure cost optimization.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The team culture is highly inclusive and encourages innovation. During the project's sprint phases, everyone supported each other relentlessly. This gave me a profound sense of belonging.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The program provides excellent flexibility and resources (such as providing AWS practice accounts), giving me the chance to gain "hands-on" experience without limitations.

### Additional Questions
- What did you find **most satisfying** during your internship?  
Being directly involved in building a highly practical product like Rookwork and getting hands-on experience with real AWS Cloud services.
- What do you think the company **should improve** for future interns?  
It would be great to have more Mock Interviews or in-depth career orientation sessions towards the end of the program.
- If recommending to a friend, would you **suggest they intern here**? Why or why not?  
Definitely. It's an excellent environment to bridge the gap between university theory and the practical demands of leading tech companies.
